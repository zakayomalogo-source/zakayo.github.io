Students' Guide

Students' Guide

How to use the student portal

📖
1️⃣ How to Register for Courses

1. Click Registration → Semester Registration
2. Choose Semester 1 or 2
3. Go to Course Registration
4. Click "Register Now" on any course
5. View in Registration History

2️⃣ How to View Grades

1. Click Academic Records → Grades
2. Filter by Semester 1 or 2
3. View transcript in Transcript
4. Print with the Print button

3️⃣ How to Take a Quiz

1. Click Field & Project → Quiz
2. Choose a subject
3. Answer all questions
4. Score is saved automatically
