Students' Guide

Students' Guide

How to use the student portal

📖
1️⃣
How to Register for Courses

1. Click Registration → Semester Registration
2. Choose Semester 1 or 2
3. Go to Course Registration
4. Click "Register" on any course
5. View your courses in Registration History

2️⃣
How to View Your Grades

1. Click Academic Records → Grades & Results
2. Filter by Semester 1 or 2
3. View your transcript in Transcript
4. Print your transcript using the Print button

3️⃣
How to Take a Quiz

1. Click Field & Project → Quiz & Tests
2. Choose a subject
3. Answer all questions
4. Your score is saved automatically
